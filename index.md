# Privacy Policy for Kya Pehnu — AI Outfit Planner
  
  **Last updated:** 20 May 2026

  Kya Pehnu ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy
  explains what data the app collects, how it is used, who it is shared with,
  and your rights regarding that data.

  By using Kya Pehnu, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---
## 1. Information We Collect

  Kya Pehnu does **not** require you to create an account. The app is designed
  to work primarily on your device, with a single exception: cloud-based AI
  analysis of photos you choose to upload.

### 1.1 Photos and Images
  When you photograph or upload an image of a clothing item:
  - The image is saved **only on your device** in the app's internal storage.
  - A copy of the image is sent to **Google Cloud Vertex AI (Gemini)** for
    automatic classification (see Section 3).
  - We do not upload, back up, or transmit your photos to any other server.

### 1.2 Wardrobe Metadata
  Item details you save (category, color, fabric, occasions, notes, captions
  generated by AI, etc.) are stored **only in a local database on your device**.
  They are not transmitted to us.

### 1.3 Usage and Diagnostic Data
  We use **Firebase Analytics** (provided by Google) to collect anonymous
  information about how the app is used:
  - Anonymous app-instance ID (auto-generated by Firebase)
  - App interactions (e.g., "item saved," "AI analysis started")
  - Crash and exception data
  - Device type, OS version, app version

  This data is **not linked to your identity** and is used to understand and
  improve the app.
  
### 1.4 What We Do NOT Collect
  - We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  - We do not access your contacts, calendar, location, or other photos.
  - We do not use accounts or login systems.
  - We do not collect payment information.

  ---

## 2. How We Use Information

  | Data | Purpose |
  |---|---|
  | Photos | Sent to Google Vertex AI to auto-classify clothing items (category, colors, fabric, occasion, styling notes, captions). |
  | Wardrobe metadata | Stored locally for you to organize, search, and view your wardrobe. |
  | Usage and diagnostic data | To understand feature usage, fix crashes, and improve the app. |

  We do **not** use your data for advertising, marketing, profile-building,
  or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

  ---

## 3. Third-Party Services

  Kya Pehnu uses the following Google services as data processors acting on
  our behalf under Google's Cloud Data Processing Addendum:

### 3.1 Google Cloud Vertex AI (Gemini)
  - **Purpose:** Analyze clothing photographs to generate category, color,
    fabric, occasion, and styling metadata.
  - **Data sent:** The clothing image you provide.
  - **Retention:** Google does **not** use your images to train AI models.
    Images are not retained beyond what is necessary to process your request,
    except that Google may log content for up to **30 days** for safety and
    abuse-monitoring purposes, in line with [Google's Vertex AI data governance
    policy](https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/generative-ai/docs/data-governance).
  - **Data sharing:** Images are not shared with any third party other than Google.

### 3.2 Firebase Analytics
  - **Purpose:** Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting.
  - **Retention:** Up to 14 months (Firebase default settings).
  - **More info:** [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### 3.3 Google Play Services
  - **Purpose:** App distribution, security, and Play Store functionality.
  - Required for the app to run on Android.

### 3.4 Google ML Kit (Subject Segmentation)
  - **Purpose:** Background removal for clothing photos.
  - **Important:** This service runs **entirely on your device**. No image
    data is transmitted to Google by ML Kit.

## 4. International Data Transfers

  Your photos and analytics data may be processed in **Google data centres
  located in the United States or other countries outside your region**.
  Google has implemented Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other
  safeguards to protect data transferred internationally, in line with GDPR
  and UK GDPR requirements.

## 6. Data Retention

  - **On your device:** Your wardrobe data and saved images remain on your
    device until you delete an item (deletes the database entry) or uninstall
    the app (deletes all data).
  - **Firebase Analytics:** Up to 14 months.
  - **Vertex AI:** Up to 30 days for abuse monitoring; otherwise not retained.

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / Similar Laws)

  If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or another
  jurisdiction with similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  - **Access** the personal data we hold about you
  - **Correct** inaccurate data
  - **Delete** your data ("right to erasure")
  - **Restrict** or **object** to processing
  - **Data portability** — receive your data in a portable format
  - **Withdraw consent** at any time
  - **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority
    (in the UK: the [Information Commissioner's Office](https://ico.org.uk))

  To exercise any right, contact us at **priyankatechlabs@gmail.com**.

  Because most of your data is stored locally on your device, the simplest
  way to delete all your data is to **uninstall the app**, which removes
  the entire local database and all saved images.

  For data processed by Google (Vertex AI analytics, Firebase Analytics),
  you may request deletion by contacting us, and we will work with Google
  to remove identifiable data where possible.

## 9. Security

  We use commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to
  protect your data:
  - Photos and database are stored in **Android's internal app storage**,
    which is sandboxed and not accessible to other apps.
  - All network requests (Vertex AI, Firebase) are transmitted over **HTTPS / TLS**.
  - We do not run any backend servers ourselves — there is no central
    database that could be breached.

  No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot
  guarantee absolute security.
